Discover Tokyo

Tokyo, Japan's bustling capital, mixes the ultramodern and the traditional, from neon-lit skyscrapers to historic temples. The opulent Meiji Shinto Shrine is known for its towering gate and surrounding woods. The Imperial Palace sits amid large public gardens. The city's many museums offer exhibits ranging from classical art to a reconstructed kabuki theater.

As the world's most populous metropolitan area, Tokyo offers an endless array of attractions. From the cutting-edge technology of Akihabara to the traditional crafts of Asakusa, from the youth fashion of Harajuku to the upscale shopping of Ginza, Tokyo is a city of fascinating contrasts. The food scene is unparalleled, with more Michelin-starred restaurants than any other city in the world.

Suggested 7-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Shinjuku

Arrive in Tokyo, check into hotel. Evening exploring Shinjuku's neon lights and Omoide Yokocho alley.

Day 2: Asakusa & Ueno

Morning at Senso-ji Temple and Nakamise shopping street. Afternoon in Ueno Park and museums.

Day 3: Harajuku & Shibuya

Explore Takeshita Street in Harajuku. Visit Meiji Shrine. Experience Shibuya Crossing in the evening.

Day 4: Day Trip to Nikko

Full day excursion to Nikko's UNESCO World Heritage shrines and beautiful nature.

Day 5: Tsukiji & Ginza

Early morning at Tsukiji Outer Market. High-end shopping in Ginza. Kabuki theater performance.

Day 6: Akihabara & Odaiba

Morning in Akihabara's electronics district. Afternoon exploring futuristic Odaiba island.

Day 7: Departure

Last minute shopping or sightseeing before departure.
