Discover Bali

Bali is an Indonesian island known for its forested volcanic mountains, iconic rice paddies, beaches and coral reefs. The island is home to religious sites such as cliffside Uluwatu Temple. To the south, the beachside city of Kuta has lively bars, while Seminyak, Sanur and Nusa Dua are popular resort towns. The island is also known for its yoga and meditation retreats.

Bali's culture is deeply spiritual and unique. The Balinese people are proud of having preserved their unique Hindu culture against the advance of Islam, the dominant religion throughout Indonesia. This is still reflected in day-to-day life and can be seen in the numerous ceremonies, Balinese temples, and offerings found all over the island. The rich culture, spectacular nature, and friendly people make Bali one of the most attractive travel destinations in the world.

Suggested 7-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Seminyak

Arrive in Bali, transfer to Seminyak. Evening exploring beach clubs and restaurants.

Day 2: Uluwatu & Jimbaran

Morning at Uluwatu Temple and beach. Sunset seafood dinner in Jimbaran Bay.

Day 3: Ubud Cultural Day

Visit Sacred Monkey Forest, Ubud Palace, and traditional art markets. Balinese dance performance.

Day 4: Tegallalang & Kintamani

Morning at Tegallalang Rice Terraces. Afternoon views of Mount Batur volcano and lake.

Day 5: Nusa Penida Day Trip

Full day excursion to Nusa Penida island with stunning beaches and cliffs.

Day 6: Tanah Lot & Spa Day

Morning visit to Tanah Lot temple. Afternoon Balinese spa treatment.

Day 7: Departure

Last minute shopping or beach time before departure.
