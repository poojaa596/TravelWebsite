Discover Rome

Rome is the capital city of Italy, a sprawling, cosmopolitan city with nearly 3,000 years of globally influential art, architecture and culture on display. Ancient ruins such as the Forum and the Colosseum evoke the power of the former Roman Empire. Vatican City, headquarters of the Roman Catholic Church, has St. Peter's Basilica and the Vatican Museums, which house masterpieces such as Michelangelo's Sistine Chapel frescoes.

Rome's history spans 28 centuries, making it one of the oldest continuously occupied cities in Europe. The city's early population originated from a mix of Latins, Etruscans, and Sabines. Rome is often referred to as the "Eternal City", a notion expressed by ancient Roman poets and writers. Today, Rome ranks among the world's most visited cities, with numerous renowned landmarks including the Pantheon, the Roman Forum, and countless churches.

Suggested 5-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Ancient Rome

Arrive in Rome, check into hotel. Afternoon at Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Palatine Hill.

Day 2: Vatican City

Full day exploring Vatican Museums, Sistine Chapel, and St. Peter's Basilica.

Day 3: Historic Center

Morning at Pantheon and Piazza Navona. Afternoon at Trevi Fountain and Spanish Steps.

Day 4: Day Trip to Pompeii

Full day excursion to ancient ruins of Pompeii with Mount Vesuvius views.

Day 5: Trastevere & Departure

Morning exploring charming Trastevere district. Afternoon free before departure.
